Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, we require a minimum of 60% marks in relevant subjects. For detailed information about specific program requirements, use our chat assistant or visit the admissions page.

Are scholarships available?

Yes, KGiSL offers various merit-based and need-based scholarships. Our scholarship programs are designed to recognize and support academic excellence and provide financial assistance to deserving students.

What is the placement record?

KGiSL has an excellent placement record with over 90% of eligible students being placed in reputed companies. Our placement cell works continuously to provide students with the best career opportunities.
